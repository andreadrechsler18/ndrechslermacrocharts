AI Impact on Professional Services Employment

Thesis: AI is not killing professional services employment

Despite widespread predictions of mass displacement, this page tracks employment in AI-exposed professions — programmers, accountants, lawyers, consultants, engineers, designers — to see if the data supports or contradicts that thesis.

Views: Raw Data shows absolute employment levels. YoY % Change shows growth rates. % of Total shows each profession's share of all nonfarm jobs. MoM and Trailing 3M show recent momentum.
